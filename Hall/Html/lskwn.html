LEE SHAU KEE Hall Welcoming Night 2019

After 2 weeks of preparing, finally LEE SHAU KEE Hall held its Welcoming Night Party. LEE SHAU KEE Hall Welcoming night is an annual event that is held at the beginning of the semester. Its purpose is to welcome new and current student, both Local and International students to the hall. This year, it is held on 17th September at Multifunctional Hall A. The event started with senior RT Muhammad giving speech and talking about the returning scheme. Then it is followed by performances by the residents of hall 6. There are singing and dancing which all performed by hall 6 residents. Also in welcoming night, all of the RTs were introduced to the residents. This year, the food for welcoming night were pizza (both halal and non halal pizza were there). This event was organized by LEE SHAU KEE Hall own residents with the help of RTs. The organizer is selected through lucky draw and extra returning points will be given to organizers. Our MC, Alvin and Eden both did very well in keeping the event going. The decoration is really stunning and the food is really delicious. The games “Form a Team” and “Jump In Jump Out” is so much fun. It was great for all of us in ice-breaking. The event was attended by many participants in which most of them are hall 6 residents. It was a really memorable event for both the organizers, since they can learn to work together with other people from different background, and for the participants since they can meet their hall mates.
