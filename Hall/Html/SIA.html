Student Initiated Activities

In Hall 6, we introduce our culture to other residents. The Student Initiated Activities act as a platform to unite residents from multicultural backgrounds by giving chance for residents to introduce their cultural uniqueness for the hall.



Tambola Night
5 February 2021


Residents had a culturally inclusive night where they were introduced to an Indian game and had a fun time witnessing the course of the game


Game Night
19 January 2021


The Residents had a fun destressing night over the latest and unique of games. Among the games were Kahoot and Name-Place-animal-thing. The winning residents were provided with starbucks and Parkenshop coupons.


Virtual Cooking Competition
14 February 2021


Hall 6 spectated a rising spurt of tallent amond the residents for the delicious food they prepared. Each resident depicted a part of their culture with the delicious food they prepared.


Python Workshop
13, 18, 20, 25 and 27 November 2020


Python is one of the commonly used programming language throughout the world. The residents learnt about general variables, datatypes, basic operators, conditionals, loops, datstructures, functional and libraries.


Virtual Language Exchange Party
12, 16, 19 November 2020


Hall 6 organised a series of cultural inclusion events where the Residents shared information about their language and taught the other residents a few phrases and greetings.


Photography Competition
15-21 November 2020


Hall 6 residents showcased their Photography tallents and the winning 3 residents received prizes from the hall.
